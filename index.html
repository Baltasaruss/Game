🕹️ Controls (PC):

• W, A, S, D / Arrows – Movement.

• Mouse – Aiming, LMB – Shot.

• Shift – Sprint, Space – Collect gasoline and scrap.

• E (or click) – Enter SUV (at camp).

• 1, 2, 3 – Switch weapon, 4, 5, 6, 7 – Items.

📱 Controls (Mobile):

• Left joystick – Movement.

• Right joystick – Aiming and Auto-fire.

• Single tap on screen – Shoot at touch point.

• 🖐️ Button – Collect resources / Enter SUV.

• ⚡ Button – Toggle sprint (run).

• Quick slots below – Weapon select and healing.
